---
title: "Aggregate demand" 
---
> [!SUMMARY]+
> Aggregate [[Demand]] (AD) is the sum of all [[Demand]] for goods and services within an economy

AD is made up of the following components:
- Consumer expenditure
- Investment
- [[Government]] expenditure
- Net exports
